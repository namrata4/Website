Women in STEM: By the numbers

A Data Analysis and Visualization Report

Although women constitute about half of the US labor force (47%), only 25% of them are currently employed in STEM. The project focuses on the current statistics for gender disparity in STEM and explore the reasons behind it along with suggesting some probable steps that could be taken to overcome the disparity
