Analysis of causal effects of Progresa

Causality Analysis in Python

Analysed causal effects of Progresa program used in Mexico in 1997 on school enrolments using counter-factual assumptions. Simple and multiple regression along with difference in differences techniques similar to those described in the paper "School subsidies for the poor: evaluating the Mexican Progresa poverty program", by Paul Shultz are used in Python to evaluate the relationships.
